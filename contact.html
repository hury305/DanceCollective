The purpose of TNS Dance Collective is to provide a space for dancers and artists alike to share a space and collaborate with one another. Through workshops led by members of the dance collective, students will be able to explore a variety of dance styles and form relationships with one another. Ready to join? Please fill out a Fitness Waiver called "Club Sport Waiver" on your NarwhalNation account and join us at our next workshop!
