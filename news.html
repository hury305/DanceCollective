Dance Collective-General Interest Meeting
Come to learn more about the Dance Collective with Captains Emily and Frank! Dance Collective is OPEN TO ALL, no prior or current experience necessary!

Date: Friday, September 7th, 2018
Location: Fitness room, UC L2. Make sure to take the two left elevators downstairs to L2!
Time: 2:30pm-3:30pm

Information: All information about the Dance Collective will be provided at the meeting. Every class participant must complete a fitness waiver every semester and have current health insurance. A complete waiver includes an insurance carrier and policy number, as well as an emergency contact, who must be someone in the United States with a U.S. phone number. If you have Aetna health insurance through The New School, you can look up your policy number by visiting aetnastudenthealth.com. Simply enter The New School, select the Print Your ID Card link, and enter your New School ID number and birthday.
